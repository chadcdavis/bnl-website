Catherine Burkhard, Instructor, Bookbinding and Calligraphy

Catherine, a Dallas, Texas resident for most of her life, has been involved in various arts and crafts groups, bookbinding and calligraphy study groups, and exhibitions. She has studied with many noted calligraphers and bookbinders, the latter including Dorothy Westapher, Dallas, Texas; Raoul Bollin, near Austin, Texas; Monique Lallier, Greensboro, North Carolina; and completed an internship at Don Etheringston's book and paper conservation center in North Carolina.

Catherine is a charter member of Kaligrafos, The Dallas Calligraphy Society and of the Lone Star Chapter of the Guild of Book Workers, currently serving as President. She also is Secretary of the national Guild of Book Workers.


Catherine frequently offers bookbinding classes and workshops. For more information, call 214.363.7946 or email bookltrs@att.
